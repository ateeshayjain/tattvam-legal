Privacy Policy — Tattvam

Last updated: 17 June 2026 Data Fiduciary / Controller: Ateeshay Jain ("we", "us", "our") Contact: ateeshay.jain@gmail.com Applies to: the Tattvam mobile application (the "App") on iOS.

Tattvam is an educational app for exploring ancient Indian philosophy. We have designed it to collect as little personal data as possible. Most of your data stays on your device. This policy explains what we collect, where it lives, what leaves your device, and the rights you have.

We are based in India, so this policy is written to comply with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDP Act") and, for users in the European Economic Area and the United Kingdom, the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR").

1. You can use Tattvam without an account

Tattvam works fully as a guest. You are not required to create an account or provide any personal information to read content, track your progress, take quizzes, journal, or play the Inner Compass game.

  • As a guest: all of your data (progress, streaks, saved quotes, journal entries, preferences, game history) is stored only on your device. None of it is sent to us or to any server.
  • With an account (optional): if you choose to sign in, a limited set of data is stored in our cloud database so it can sync and (where you opt in) power community features. Sign-in uses Apple or Google via our authentication provider (Supabase). We do not operate our own password system.

2. What we collect and where it lives

A. Data stored only on your device (never sent to us)

Stored locally using your device's app storage (AsyncStorage) and, for authentication tokens, the operating system's secure keystore (Expo SecureStore / iOS Keychain / Android Keystore):

  • Reading and layer progress, completed questions, daily-question state.
  • Gamification state when used as a guest (XP, streaks, ranks, quote-guessing history, daily quests, quote-collection progress, and stances you take on philosophical tensions).
  • Inner Compass game run history.
  • Saved quotes and journal entries when used as a guest.
  • App preferences (e.g. simplified/scholarly reading mode, selected themes, notification settings, language).
  • Your optional, self-supplied Anthropic (Claude) API key — see Section 4.
  • Your authentication session tokens, if you sign in (held in the secure keystore, not in normal app storage).

We cannot see this data. It is removed if you delete the App or clear its data.

B. Data stored in our cloud database (only if you create an account)

We use Supabase (project reference dkjlludfdfmfgzxfdapr) as our hosted database and authentication backend. When you sign in, the following may be stored, each row protected so that you can only read or write your own records (Row-Level Security):

Table What it holds
user_profiles A display name, optional avatar URL, wisdom XP, current/longest streak, last-active date, and your selected themes. The display name and avatar come from the Apple/Google account you sign in with, or default to "Seeker".
user_progress Which questions and layers you have completed and XP earned.
user_journal_entries Your private written reflections. Private to you — readable and writable only by you.
user_collections Quotes you have saved. Private to you.
user_reactions Your Agree / Disagree / Hmm reactions on certain content items.
community_reflections A reflection you have explicitly chosen to share with the community (opt-in). See Section 3.
sabha_rooms / sabha_members / sabha_events If you join a live Sabhā multiplayer session: the room code, an anonymized handle, and the game-event log (choices in the game). No reflection or journal text is ever stored here, and stale rooms are automatically deleted within about 2 days.

We also receive, from our authentication provider at sign-in, the basic account identifier and profile fields (name, email-derived identifier, avatar) that Apple or Google passes through to create your account.

We do not collect your contacts, photos, precise location, advertising identifiers, or browsing history. We do not use any analytics or tracking SDK. We do not track you across other apps or websites.

If crash reporting is enabled in a given release, anonymous crash reports (stack trace, device model, OS version — configured to exclude personal content and request data) are sent to Sentry to help us fix bugs. Crash reports are not used for advertising or profiling.

C. Bundled content

The philosophical content in the App (questions, quotes, translations) is shipped inside the App as static files. It contains no personal data and does not require a network connection to read.

3. Community Reflections (opt-in, anonymous)

On certain reflection screens you may choose to share a written reflection with other users. This is off by default and happens only when you actively tap "Share".

When you share, your reflection text is stored in the community_reflections table linked to your account internally so that you can later edit or withdraw it. However, when other users view community reflections, they are served through a special read-only database function (a SECURITY DEFINER function) that returns only an aggregate count and anonymized excerpts — it never exposes your user ID or any identifier. Other users cannot tell who wrote a reflection.

You can withdraw a shared reflection at any time, which removes it from public view. Please do not include personal or sensitive information in text you choose to share.

4. The "Ask About This" AI chat (optional, bring-your-own key)

The App includes an optional AI chat feature. To use it, you supply your own Anthropic (Claude) API key, which is stored only on your device.

  • When you send a chat message, your message and the relevant question context are sent directly from your device to Anthropic's API (api.anthropic.com) using your key. The data does not pass through our servers.
  • Anthropic's handling of that data is governed by Anthropic's own privacy policy and terms, which apply to your use of your own key. Please review them at anthropic.com.
  • We never receive your API key or your chat content.

If you do not add an API key, this feature is inactive and no chat data leaves your device.

5. Notifications

The App can show local reminders (e.g. a daily-practice nudge) that are scheduled on your device. We do not run a push-notification server and we do not collect or transmit device push tokens. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

6. Sharing to WhatsApp / other apps

If you share a question to WhatsApp or another app, the App opens that app with a pre-filled text message via a system link. No personal data is sent to us in this process; what you share, and with whom, is controlled entirely by you in the other app.

8. Purposes and legal bases for processing

We process personal data only to:

  • provide and maintain the App and your account;
  • save and sync your progress and saved content;
  • power opt-in community reflections;
  • keep the service secure and prevent abuse.

Under GDPR, our legal bases are performance of a contract (providing the App and account you requested), consent (for optional features such as community sharing and notifications, withdrawable at any time), and our legitimate interests in operating and securing the service. Under the DPDP Act, we process personal data on the basis of your consent and for the specified purposes above.

9. Data retention

  • Local on-device data persists until you delete it or uninstall the App.
  • Cloud account data is retained while your account exists. If you ask us to delete your account, we delete your associated rows (profile, progress, journal, collections, reactions, and reflections). Account deletion cascades from your authentication record by design.

10. Children's data

Tattvam is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Under the DPDP Act, processing the data of a child (under 18 in India) requires verifiable parental consent; we do not knowingly process such data and ask that minors use guest mode (which stores nothing with us) or obtain a parent's/guardian's consent before creating an account. Under GDPR, the App is not intended for children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

11. International transfers

If you create an account, your account data is stored on Supabase's hosted infrastructure, which may be located outside your country (including outside India or the EEA). Where required, such transfers are made with appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses, where applicable). Guest data never leaves your device and is not transferred.

12. Security

Account data is protected by Row-Level Security so that only you can read or write your own records. Authentication tokens are stored in your device's secure keystore. Network connections use HTTPS. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we apply reasonable, industry-standard safeguards.

13. Your rights

You can exercise the following rights. Many are also available directly in the App.

Everyone (in-app):

  • Access / portability: your on-device data is yours; use Profile → Export My Data in the App to export everything at any time (or contact us).
  • Erasure: use Profile → Delete Account in the App to permanently erase your account and all associated cloud data at any time; deleting the App or clearing its data removes local data.
  • Withdraw consent: turn off notifications, withdraw shared community reflections, or remove your AI API key at any time.

Under the DPDP Act (India), you have the right to access a summary of your personal data and processing, to correction and erasure, to grievance redressal, and to nominate another person to exercise your rights in the event of death or incapacity.

Under GDPR (EEA/UK), you have the rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection, and the right to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority.

To exercise any right, or to raise a grievance, contact us at ateeshay.jain@gmail.com. We will respond within the timelines required by applicable law.
